Museums in Nairobi & Mombasa

Nairobi National Museum

Natural history, culture, and art exhibits.

Karen Blixen Museum

Home of the author of “Out of Africa”.

Railway Museum Nairobi

Historic trains and the story of the “Lunatic Express”.

Kenya National Archives

History and photography collection on Moi Avenue.

Bomas of Kenya (Cultural)

Traditional homesteads and live dances.

Fort Jesus Museum – Mombasa

UNESCO site telling coastal history.

Jumba la Mtwana Ruins

Coastal Swahili settlement ruins north of Mombasa.

Lamu Museum & Swahili House

Swahili culture and maritime history in Lamu.

Gede (Gedi) Ruins Museum

Mysterious 12th‑century Swahili town near Watamu.
